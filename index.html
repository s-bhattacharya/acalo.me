About

A lawyer who teaches the law.

I'm Sayon Bhattacharya — a practicing corporate lawyer and one of the highest-scoring LSAT tutors available anywhere. I scored 174 in 2021 and returned to score 176 in 2022, placing in the 99th percentile on both attempts.

I didn't take the LSAT to apply to law school. I took it because I was fascinated by how precisely it mirrors the logical reasoning that legal practice actually demands. That curiosity — and the legal training behind it — is what I bring to every student.
